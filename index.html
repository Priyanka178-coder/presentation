Stay Safe Online

The internet is a powerful tool, but it also comes with risks. Understanding how to stay safe online is essential in today’s digital world.

Protect Your Info

Never share personal details like passwords, bank info, or OTPs with anyone. Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts.
